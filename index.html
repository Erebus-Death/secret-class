About the Manhwa

Secret Class is a South Korean manhwa (웹툰) that has garnered a large following for its blend of drama, romance, and mature themes. Written by Wang Kang-cheol and illustrated by Minachan, the series explores the coming-of-age story of Daeho as he navigates complex relationships within the household that raised him.
